- **Data silos:** Legacy systems are monolithic, lack modern APIs
- **Cost:** 59% lack financial capacity for AI
- **Skills gap:** 57% lack in-house technical expertise
- **Compliance:** RBI mandates data localization for financial data. DPDPA 2023 enables government to restrict cross-border data transfers
- **60% of DIY AI initiatives fail to scale past pilot** (industry data)
- **95% of GenAI initiatives fail to deliver measurable ROI** (MIT research)
