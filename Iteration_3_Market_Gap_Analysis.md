# Iteration 3: Market Gap Analysis & Strategic Positioning

*Building on the competitive breakdown from Iteration 2, this document identifies the specific market gaps Quellix can exploit as a 1-person, pure-digital engineering team.*

---

## The Three Market Gaps

### Gap 1: The "Pilot Purgatory" Crisis
- 95% of GenAI initiatives fail to deliver measurable ROI (MIT). 60% of DIY AI fail to scale past pilot.
- Companies build demos that work in isolation but can't integrate into actual digital workflows.
- **Quellix opportunity:** Nobody is selling "We rescue your failed AI pilot." This is pure digital engineering — reading codebases, fixing API integrations, refactoring data pipelines.

### Gap 2: The "Integration Depth" Void
- 71% of enterprise applications remain disconnected. MSMEs cite legacy integration and data silos as primary barriers.
- Broad agencies sell "new AI apps." Most businesses need AI embedded into tools they already use (Zendesk, HubSpot, Xero).
- **Quellix opportunity:** Build API middleware between existing SaaS tools using n8n/Make + LangChain, deploy in 4 weeks at $8K–$25K.

### Gap 3: The "Unstructured Data" Bottleneck
- 80–90% of enterprise data is unstructured (PDFs, invoices, contracts). IDP market growing at 26–30% CAGR.
- Big IDP vendors sell massive annual-license platforms. Small businesses can't afford them.
- **Quellix opportunity:** Custom, lightweight document processing pipelines using GPT-4o or AWS Textract, wired via n8n directly into client's Finance/HR SaaS. Fixed-outcome pricing.
